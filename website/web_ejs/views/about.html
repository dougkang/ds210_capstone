How does Instaplaces create personalized recommendations for travelers?

Our models consist of a feature extraction component and a clustering component. The models are trained and evaluated in parallel. They take a collection of posts and outputs a list of top locations sorted by confidence. An online linear computation component combines the models based on predefined weights.

The image models utilize Caffe, an image processing library with pretrained image recognition models. The pretrained models Instaplaces uses detect objects and scenes from Instagram images.

How can you get travel recommendations?

You must have a valid Instagram account with at least ten photos posted. Fewer photos may result in inaccurate results. Click on the home page.

Instaplaces will show you the top destinations in order of decreasing confidence and corresponding pictures from each destination. Also, Instagram users who are similar to you are displayed.
